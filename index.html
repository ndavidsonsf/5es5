My Approach

Depth, insight,
and the here and now.

My work is rooted in depth psychology — the belief that lasting change comes from understanding the unconscious forces, early experiences, and relational patterns that shape us. I draw on psychoanalytic thinking, Jungian frameworks, and transpersonal psychology to help you understand the full arc of your story.

At the same time, I am firmly present with you in the here and now — listening, reflecting, and holding a compassionate space for whatever you bring. I believe that the therapeutic relationship itself is a profound vehicle for healing.
